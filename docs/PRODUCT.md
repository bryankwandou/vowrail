# Product brief

## Positioning

Most agency systems store policy records. Vowrail governs the moments where an unsupported decision creates Errors and Omissions exposure: binding, claims intake, and renewal follow-through.

## Locked identity

**Vowrail** combines the promise implied by a policy with the enforced path implied by a rail. The name is short, pronounceable, and broad enough to support a category beyond the initial insurance workflow.

## Business model

- Primary buyer: independent agency principal or operations lead.
- Initial wedge: pre-bind hard blocks for policy types with recurring evidence gaps.
- Expansion: renewal operations, claims intake governance, multi-office controls, carrier-facing evidence exports.
- Pricing hypothesis: platform fee by agency plus active-policy tiers; no commission or premium percentage.

## Competitive defense

The defensible asset is not a generic dashboard or AI summary. It is the agency-specific requirement graph, immutable decision history, and outcome data that reveal where coverage operations repeatedly fail.

## MVP limits

This release does not rate policies, calculate commissions, file directly with carriers, or automate jurisdiction-specific compliance rules.
