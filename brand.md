# Vowrail Brand System

## Position

Vowrail is the binding-integrity and renewal-control layer for independent insurance agencies. It is not an agency management system, CRM, quoting engine, or generic AI assistant. It governs the specific decisions that create preventable Errors and Omissions exposure.

## Mark

The mark combines a shield with a contract nib. The shield signals protection; the amber center seam signals a decision that becomes binding only after evidence is present.

## Palette

- Control ink `#101828`: primary text and permanent records.
- Operational paper `#F6F7F9`: calm workspace surface.
- Evidence blue `#2457D6`: verified controls and primary actions.
- Exception amber `#D88A12`: approaching risk and elevated authority.
- Block red `#C73737`: a prevented unsafe transition.
- Verified green `#14805E`: fulfilled evidence and confirmed outcomes.

All status colors are paired with explicit text. Body copy uses control ink or muted slate at WCAG AA contrast on white and operational paper.

## Typography

Manrope is used for high-information headings. Inter is used for controls, records, and body copy. Dense operational data stays compact without becoming visually timid.

## Voice

Direct, sober, and specific. Vowrail says what is blocked, what evidence is missing, who has authority, and what happens next. It never uses magical automation language or implies that AI replaces licensed judgment.

## Motion

Motion explains state changes only: loading rotation, evidence completion, and escalation feedback. Reduced-motion preferences remove nonessential transitions.
