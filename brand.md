# Vowrail Brand System

Vowrail is the notary at the edge of autonomous commerce. It should feel exact, calm, and difficult to bypass.

## Voice

- Direct, short, operational.
- Prefer inspect, bind, deny, settle, verify.
- Never use magical, revolutionary, seamless, intelligent, or next-generation.
- Never promise absolute safety.

## Palette

- Ink `#11110f`
- Paper `#f1efe7`
- Panel `#e7e4d9`
- Signal `#c8ff2e`
- Chain `#3857ff`
- Alert `#ff5c35`
- Muted `#6f7069`

## Mark

A square policy boundary with a diagonal checkpoint cut. The interruption represents an action stopped for inspection; the lime bar represents approval after validation.
